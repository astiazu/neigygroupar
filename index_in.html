    Regional and National Transformation

    The development of a 7G communication system, together with the implementation of local municipal districts and their integration with regional productive economies through the "Capicop" concept, are key elements to drive regional development across provinces.

    These initiatives are essential suggestions to achieve a Political Agreement in Argentina, enabling progress toward a more mature, developed, productive, and equitable nation. This agreement will only be possible through the consensus of all stakeholders.

    To achieve this, the following actions are proposed:

    General Proposals Creation of New Regional Companies

    It is proposed to establish companies that provide genuine employment in each region. These companies will operate as Temporary Joint Ventures (U.T.E.), financed by managers or directors, including current or former politicians. This model will enable the legalization of concealed capital, reinvesting it responsibly in regional development.

    Democratic Modernization

    To enhance the political system and reduce expenses, the following reforms are proposed:

    • Reduce the number of deputies and senators to 1910 levels.
    • Review and terminate politically appointed permanent employees from 1983 to date.
    Tax and Administrative Reform

    Replace the 170 existing taxes with a single tax applicable to producers of goods and services. Additionally, advanced security technologies such as anti-hacker 7G networks and fingerprint-enabled transaction cards will be implemented for greater safety.

    Specific Proposals Infrastructure and Environment

    Developing modern infrastructure is critical for sustainable growth. The proposals include:

    • Construction of aqueducts, sewage systems, and underground communication networks to reduce visual pollution.
    • Eradicate open-pit mining, which harms tourist and natural ecosystems.
    Transport and Road Safety

    Introduce new control technologies and vehicle homologation, including autonomous vehicles, to improve road safety and reduce accidents on Argentina's highways.

    Judicial System and Human Rights

    Replace capital punishment with sealed educational modules that foster reintegration in a humanitarian and rights-respecting environment.

    National Sovereignty

    Adopt MTM (Malvinas Time Meridian) as the new time zone, as part of a strategy to negotiate the sovereignty of the Malvinas Islands, Georgias, and other territories in the South Atlantic.
